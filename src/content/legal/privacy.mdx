# Privacy Policy 

Last updated: May 18, 2025

**We believe privacy is a fundamental human right.** This site does not sell your personal information to any third parties. All settings (like if you've set a specific theme) are stored within your browser's local storage.

We do not use any trackers on this site. However, we may see aggregated data on what pages you visit, if you use Google search. If there is a tracker on our site though, please email us at [hello@queerwinnipeg.ca](mailto:hello@queerwinnipeg.ca?subject=Tracker%20Found) so we can fix that. We host this site on Cloudflare Pages, with analytics turned off.

We also have links to third parties, which have their own privacy policies.

This privacy policy may change at any time, with or without notice.
