This is a curation of cool stuff in Winnipeg.

## Events

- [Ai-Kon](https://ai-kon.org/) - Annual anime convention, including a local market, panels, and cosplay.
- [Skullspace (Open House)](https://skullspace.ca) - Socialize with people who like tech, happens every Tuesday at 6 pm, ending vaguely around 9.
- [Pride Winnipeg Festival](https://pridewinnipeg.com/) - Yearly pride march, including a market.
- [Umbrella Boxing](https://umbrellaboxing.com/) - Pay-what-you-want boxing for the queer community, roughly every two weeks.
- [Winnipeg Punk Rock Flea Market](https://winnipegpunkrockfleamarket.com/) - Local market with lots of like-minded artists. Happens twice a year.
- [WINNISEC](https://winnisec.life/) - Small cybersecurity conference, in-person at Skullspace every 4th Friday of the month. Runs off a donation "pay-what-you-can" model.

## Economic Supports

- [Free Tax Clinics](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/campaigns/free-tax-help.html) - Free tax clinics for low-income families and individuals. Not for the self-employed, or otherwise has a more complicated tax situation.
- [RaY Inc.](https://rayinc.ca) - For people under 30 years old, open-house for free food, laundry, job, and housing services.
- [Supporting Winnipeggers - Winnipeg.ca](https://legacy.winnipeg.ca/Interhom/SupportingWinnipeggers/default.stm) - All services provided to people in Winnipeg that are free or subsidized.

## Health

- [Canada Strong Mask](https://canadastrongmask.ca/) - Comfortable, quality masks that don't fog up glasses.
- [Klinic Community Health](https://klinic.mb.ca) - STI testing, trans health, and various counseling services (including drop-in) for free.
- [Manitoba Family Doctor Finder](https://www.gov.mb.ca/health/familydoctorfinder/) - Find a primary care provider in Manitoba.
- [Winnipeg Walk-In Clinics](https://wrha.mb.ca/wait-times/walk-in-clinics/) - Find a walk-in clinic to address most health care issues you may have.

## Places

- [Club 200](https://www.facebook.com/profile.php/?id=100028283545563) - Small queer community bar, with a pool table and dance floor. Serves a small menu with great food.
- [Rainbow Resource Centre](https://rainbowresourcecentre.org/) - Hang-out events and counseling for the queer community.
- [Recreation & Leisure - Winnipeg.ca](https://www.winnipeg.ca/recreation-leisure) - Publicly-funded fitness and leisure centres, arenas and rinks, pools, and more. Some free, some paid; see link for details.
- [Select Start](https://www.selectstartwpg.com/) - Cyberpunk-themed barcade in the downtown exchange district.
- [Skullspace](https://skullspace.ca/) - Winnipeg hackerspace/makerspace for members (paid). Open house is free every Tuesday at 6 pm.
- [Winnipeg Public Libraries](https://www.winnipeg.ca/recreation-leisure/libraries) - Find your local library for a good place to work, study, or access various forms of media such as audiobooks, paper books, eBooks, movies, music, video games, and more for free.
