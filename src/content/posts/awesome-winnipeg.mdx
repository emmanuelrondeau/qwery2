This is a curation of cool stuff in Winnipeg, created by and for Winnipeggers. Please help contribute to this list!

## Economic Supports

- [Free Tax Clinics](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/campaigns/free-tax-help.html) - Free tax clinics for low-income families and individuals. Not for the self-employed, or otherwise has a more complicated tax situation.
- [Harvest Manitoba](https://www.harvestmanitoba.ca/) - Food bank, accessible through registering via their website. Formerly known as Winnipeg Harvest.
- [RaY Inc.](https://rayinc.ca) - For individuals under 30 years old, open-house for free food, laundry, job, and housing services.
- [Sunshine House](https://www.sunshinehousewpg.org/) - A community drop-in and resource centre, similar to Ray Inc. Offers safe sex supplies, and has Naloxone on-site.

- [Supporting Winnipeggers - Winnipeg.ca](https://legacy.winnipeg.ca/Interhom/SupportingWinnipeggers/default.stm) - All services provided to individuals in Winnipeg that are free or subsidized.

## Events

- [Ai-Kon](https://ai-kon.org/) - Annual anime convention, including a local market, panels, and cosplay. Has entry fee.
- [Skullspace (Open House)](https://skullspace.ca) - Socialize with individuals who like tech, happens every Tuesday at 6 pm, ending vaguely around 9.
- [Pride Winnipeg Festival](https://pridewinnipeg.com/) - Yearly pride march, including a market.
- [Umbrella Boxing](https://umbrellaboxing.com/) - Pay-what-you-want boxing for the queer community, roughly every two weeks.
- [Winnipeg Punk Rock Flea Market](https://winnipegpunkrockfleamarket.com/) - Local market with lots of like-minded artists. Happens twice a year.
- [WINNISEC](https://winnisec.life/) - Small cybersecurity conference, in-person at Skullspace every 4th Friday of the month. Runs off a donation "pay-what-you-can" model.

## Health

- [Canada Strong Mask](https://canadastrongmask.ca/) - Comfortable, quality masks that don't fog up glasses.
- [Klinic Community Health](https://klinic.mb.ca) - STI testing, trans health, and various counseling services (including drop-in) for free.
- [Manitoba Family Doctor Finder](https://www.gov.mb.ca/health/familydoctorfinder/) - Find a primary care provider in Manitoba.
- [Winnipeg Walk-In Clinics](https://wrha.mb.ca/wait-times/walk-in-clinics/) - Find a walk-in clinic to address most health care issues you may have.
